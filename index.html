James Gosling

Born in 1955

Calgary, Canada

Also known as "Dr. Java", James Gosling is a Canadian computer scientist, creator of the Java programming language. He got the idea for the Java VM while writing a project.

Gosling's relevant events:

  • 1977 Received a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Calgary.
  • 1983 Received a M.A. & Ph.D. in Computer Science from Carneige Mellon University.
  • 1984 Invented an Unix interface with Sun Microsystems.
  • 1994 Created the original design of Java.
  • 2007 Made an Officer of the Order of Canada.
  • 2010 Left Sun Microsystems.
  • 2015 Awarded IEEE Jhon von Neumann Medal.
  • 2019 Named a Computer History Museum Fellow for the creation of Java.
